Appendix

Common Terms

Term Meaning
Affix A Special Ability found on a Weapon or Unit.
Recipe An affix simulation that maps what fodders you need to prepare or Special Abilities needed.
Fodder Refers to the units or weapons that make up the Special Abilities desired on main item.
Direct Slot Affix made using the base number of Special Abilities on the desired main item.
Upslot Affix with the purpose of increasing the number of Special Abilities on a main item.
Downslot Affix made intentionally or unintentionally done by failing one or more Special Abilities on a main item.
Dudu Day Specific Special Ability type success rate boost for a day.
Affix Week/Boost Week Universal Special Ability success rate boost for a limited time.
